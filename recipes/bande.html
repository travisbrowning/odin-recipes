Bacon and Eggs

Description

This shit is simple and litterally tastes good all the time.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs.
  • Bacon.
  • Avocado.
  • Cherry tomatoes.
  • Salt and pepper.
  • Everything but the bagel seasoning.

Steps

  1. Start frying as much bacon you can eat without feeling embarrased.
  2. Protip: Cook it in the over at 350 until it looks good as fuck.
  3. Fry or scramble 2 eggs depending on how you're feeling, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Quarter and peel an avocado, apply the everything seasoning.
  5. Cut your tamatoes in half, you can let them cook with the bacon or eat them raw.
  6. Eat.
