Wings

Description

These simple air fryer wings are great for game day or any day!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of chicken wings.
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, baking powder.
  • Franks Red Hot.
  • Butter.

Steps

  1. Bring the wings to room temp and pat them dry.
  2. Mix your dry ingredients, just eyeball it, and coat your wings.
  3. Place wings in airfryer at 400 for 10 minutes, flip and continue to cook for 10 more minutes.
  4. Add a tablespoon of butter and as much Franks as you can handle in a large bowl.
  5. Place wings in the bowl and toss.
