Pork Chops and green beans.

Delicious and simple pork chops and green beans.

Make these delicious and simple pork chops and green beans in as little as 15 minutes! Keto and Paleo friendly, these pork chops filling and healthy and will be a staple for your dinners on a busy work week night.

Ingredients

  • 1 Butcher Box Pork loin chop
  • 1 can organic green beans
  • 1 egg
  • Umami seasoning
  • Organic BBQ sauce
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Marinate your pork job in a plastic sandwhich bag with the organic bbq sauce of your choice. Try to let it marinate for atleast an hour.
  2. Salt and pepper the marinated pork chop and pop it in the air fryer at 350 degrees, flipping once at the half way mark.
  3. While the pork chop is cooking, open your can of green beans and pour into a small pot, add umami seasoning to taste and cook at medium high until they start to boil the reduce to simmer.
  4. As the pork chop finishes let it rest for 10 minutes and fry an egg over medium.
  5. Place the green beans in a bowl and add the fried egg to the side of it (you can add sriracha as a treat), cut the pork chop and place over the green beans and you're good to go!
