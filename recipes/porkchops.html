Pork Chops and Green Beans

Description

Simple pork chops and green beans for any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • Thick pork chop.
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Can of green beans.
  • Mushrooms.
  • Egg.
  • BBQ Sauce.
  • Sriracha.

Steps

  1. Let your pork chop marinate in the BBQ sauce for at least an hour and bring it to room temp.
  2. Put green beans and mushrooms in a small pot, season to taste, and let them simmer.
  3. Salt and pepper your pork chop, place in airfryer at 350 for 15 minutes, flip once, let rest for 5 minutes and slice.
  4. Fry an egg over easy.
  5. Place cooked green beans/mushrooms in a bowl, then the sliced porkchop, then the fried egg.
  6. Put a cute little swirl of sriracha on it.
